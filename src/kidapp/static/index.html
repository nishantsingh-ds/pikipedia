Frequently Asked Questions

Is KidApp safe for children?
Yes! All content is checked by our AI safety agent to ensure it's age-appropriate and free from harmful or complex language.
Can I use KidApp as a teacher or parent?
Absolutely! KidApp is designed for kids, parents, and educators. Use it to spark curiosity, explain tough topics, or create fun learning moments.
What kinds of images can I upload?
You can upload photos of animals, objects, plants, or scenes. Please avoid personal photos or anything not suitable for children.
How long does it take to get results?
Usually 30-60 seconds. Complex questions or large images may take a bit longer.
Is my data private?
We do not store your questions, images, or results. All processing is done securely and privately.
